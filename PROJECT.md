# Project: Structural Engineering Consulting Website

## Overview
This project is a professional website for an independent structural engineer offering consulting services to architects, developers, and MEP engineers.

The goal is to establish credibility, generate leads, and convert visitors into paying clients for plan reviews, constructability feedback, and engineering consulting.

---

## Business Context

The business starts as a solo operation (moonlighting) and may later transition into an LLC.

### Services Offered:
- Structural Plan Review
- Constructability Review
- Engineering Consulting
- Coordination with Architects & MEP Engineers
- AI-Assisted Design Feedback (future offering)

### Target Audience:
- Architects (primary)
- MEP Engineers
- Small to mid-sized developers
- Contractors needing quick expert feedback

---

## Goals

### Phase 1 (MVP)
- Establish online presence
- Capture leads via contact form
- Clearly communicate services and credibility
- Enable quick inquiry submission

### Phase 2
- Add case studies / past projects
- Add pricing or “request a quote” flow
- SEO optimization for local traffic (Florida)
- Add blog or insights section

### Phase 3
- Transition branding to LLC
- Add client portal or dashboards
- Integrate scheduling and payment system

---

## Core Pages

### 1. Home
- Clear value proposition
- Services overview
- CTA (Request Consultation)
- Trust signals (experience, certifications)

### 2. Services
- Detailed breakdown:
  - Plan Review
  - Constructability Feedback
  - Consulting
- Benefits and outcomes

### 3. About
- Engineer’s background
- Credentials (PE license, experience)
- Personal credibility

### 4. Contact
- Simple form:
  - Name
  - Email
  - Company
  - Project description
- CTA: “Request Consultation”

### 5. (Optional) Projects / Case Studies
- Before/after insights
- Problems solved
- Results
